Tacos

A taco is a traditional Mexican food consisting of a small hand-sized corn- or wheat-based tortilla topped with a filling. The tortilla is then folded around the filling and eaten by hand.

Ingredients

  • 18 medium taco shells
  • 2 lbs lean ground beef
  • 1 bottle ketchup
  • 1 package shredded cheese
  • 1 large tomato
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce

Steps

  1. Warm taco shells
  2. In a medium skillet over medium high heat, brown the beef. Halfway through browning, pour in ketchup. Stir well and let simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Spoon the meat mixture into the warm taco shells and top with Cheddar cheese.
  4. Return the filled taco shells to the preheated oven and bake until cheese is melted.
  5. Top each taco with a little tomato and lettuce.
